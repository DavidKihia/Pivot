Policies

Please make sure to read the Policies carefully.

1. Rent deposit is refundable one month after expiry of tenancy period and after the caretaker has inspected the premise. Incase you have damaged any property, your deposit will be deducted according to the damage caused.

2. Any tenant who vacates the house without notice, shall not qualify for deposit refund because of tresspass and breaching protocal.

3. The tenant shall not assign sublet or part with possession of the premise or make any addition of unknown occupants to the premises without caretakers knowledge or consent.

4. The tenant will allow only the caretaker to inspect the premise at any reasonable time of the day 8:00 am- 5:00pm incase of complains and other necessities

5. Keep every part of the compound as tidy as possible. CLEANLINESS IS NEXT TO GODLINESS.

6. Use of the house area for purposes of trade is prohibited unless the description says so.

7. It is prohibeted to manufacture or be in possession of consummation or sale of any intoxication liquor.

8. Any personal property left outside the room unattended to especially during the night will be at owners risk.

9. The management reserves the right to revise the tenancy agreement to cater for any changes in cost and rules antimere.



Rules and Regulations

1. Jumping over the gate and fences is prohibited.

2. Tenants should not act or cause annoyance to others e.g Loud Music, shouting and gossiping and incitment since its an offence.

3. It is prohibited to keep dogs, chicken, pigeons, or other animals except cats and unless authorised by the landlord.

4. Be reminded that all types of transactions shall be being carried out through the caretaker or landlord only.

5. Ensure proper disposal of kitchen refuse, Sanitary towels and Diapers to prevent blockages.

6. Tenants shall make no alterations to the house or construct any building or make other improvents without prior written consent of the Landlord.

7. Tenants shall not keep or have on or around the house any article or thing that is dangerous, inflammable or expensive that might unreasonably increase the danger of fire on or around the house or that might be considered harzodous.


NOTE: Payment of Rent should be done duely before the 10th day of every month. Failure to this will attract penalties of Ksh.100 per each day surpassed.
